About This Application

Transforming customer research with AI-powered interview simulations

What is AI-Powered Customer Discovery?

This application leverages advanced AI technologies to simulate dozens or hundreds of customer interviews simultaneously. By creating diverse, realistic personas and conducting in-depth conversations with them, the system can quickly identify key customer pain points, needs, and opportunities.

Traditional customer discovery can be time-consuming and limited by the number of interviews that can be reasonably conducted. Our AI approach allows you to scale your research and gain deeper insights without the logistical challenges of traditional methods.

How It Works

1. Define Context

Provide information about your product, service, or research area.

2. Generate Personas

AI creates diverse, realistic user personas relevant to your context.

3. Simulate Interviews

AI conducts multiple parallel conversations with these personas.

4. Extract Insights

Key themes and pain points are identified across all conversations.

5. Present Findings

Insights are aggregated and presented in an actionable format.

Technical Architecture

This application is built using a combination of advanced technologies:

  • Claude 3.7 Sonnet: Used for creating detailed, realistic personas with complex characteristics
  • GPT-4-o-mini: Powers the interview conversations and insight extraction
  • Flask: Backend API handling simulation management and data processing
  • Modern JavaScript: Frontend interface for real-time interaction and visualization

Benefits

  • Scale: Conduct dozens or hundreds of interviews simultaneously
  • Speed: Get results in minutes instead of weeks
  • Diversity: Access a wider range of personas than typically possible
  • Cost-effectiveness: Reduce the expense of traditional research
  • Depth: Ask follow-up questions that probe deeper into motivations
  • Actionable Insights: Receive organized, prioritized findings
